Crystals Collector!

  • You're given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your total score.
  • To win the game, match your total score to the random number. If your total score goes above the random number, you will lose.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
  • The values will change for each crystal each time a new game starts. Good luck!
